Kahless the Unforgettable

Kahless is the great unifier of the Klingon people. He is the first to have used a bat'leth, which he made from a lock of his own hair forged in a volcano. Kahless was both a great warrior and a tremendous leader, unifying Klingons at war with one another so that they could conquer the stars.

Kotar and Lunob, the first Klingons

Kotar and his mate, Lunob, were the first Klingons. Their hearts were forged by the gods themselves in a place called QI'tu'. Their love for one another was so great that they destroyed the gods who created them and burned the heavens. Kotar's punishment for was to ferry the souls of the dishonored dead to Gre'Thor where they will be tortured by Fek'lhr for all eternity. Their story is recounted and celebrated in every Klingon wedding ceremony. "No one can oppose the beating of two Klingon hearts."

Fek'lhr

Fek'lhr is the mythical Guardian of Gre'thor, tasked with watching over (torturing) the souls of the dishonored dead for all eternity. Fek'lhr is a demonic figure and is said to have a cadre of minions who aid him in his reign over the the place of dishonor.
