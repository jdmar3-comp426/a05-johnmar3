Stovokor

When a Klingon dies honorably in combat, they take their place among the honored dead in Stovokor. This is why Klingons treat death in battle as having the utmost importance. Those who did not die in battle can be redeemed by comrades who dedicate a battle won so that they may take their place alongside the honored dead in Stovokor.

Gre'thor

Gre'thor is the domain of Fek'lhr and the eternal home of the dishonored dead. After being ferried to the underworld by Kotar, Fek'lhr greets those who died without honor at the gates to usher them into an eternity of torture and agony.

QI'tu'

The place where the gods created Kotar and his mate, Lunob: the first Klingons.
